## Overview 

Once you have reviewed your Pending Changes, it's time to deploy those changes in your AWS Org. 

---

## Deploying Your Changes

1. Log in to your AWS Org & navigate to CloudFormation

2. Click <button>Create Stack</button>

3. Upload the CloudFormation template downloaded from Sonrai's Cloud Permission Firewall

<img src="/img/cpf/permissions-firewall/pending-changes/deploying-changes/5A4B17E1-D881-4773-8533-4A151AE60DFB.png"></img>

4. Provide the stack a name of your choosing

<img src="/img/cpf/permissions-firewall/pending-changes/deploying-changes/B06E39ED-2754-4D5E-A9C0-E6C5CF0ABBCB.png"></img>

5. Click <orangebutton>Next</orangebutton>, making no changes

<img src="/img/cpf/permissions-firewall/pending-changes/deploying-changes/00019887-FBEA-44F0-9B03-9380143036CD.png"></img>

6. Review and create, clicking <orangebutton>Submit</orangebutton> (making no further changes)

<img src="/img/cpf/permissions-firewall/pending-changes/deploying-changes/4648B028-9DE1-4A64-9BF9-7B19A1E43CC8.png"></img>
<img src="/img/cpf/permissions-firewall/pending-changes/deploying-changes/952C1E52-C623-47AA-BBDC-528E2AFA0967.png"></img>

7. Review the newly created stack & policies

<img src="/img/cpf/permissions-firewall/pending-changes/deploying-changes/BAB3376B-785B-4226-BFD6-1BF55F4084CE.png"></img>

<img src="/img/cpf/permissions-firewall/pending-changes/deploying-changes/51C22182-9233-4052-8418-03D352BEBF38.png"></img>
