## Overview 

Sonrai's Cloud Permissions Firewall will enable your teams to keep development pace without sacrificing on security best practices: 
- control access to the most sensitive permissions within your cloud 
- integrate security into DevOps workflows, making cloud security a seamless experience

With a view of cloud activity from the past 30 days across all accounts within your AWS Organization, our Cloud Permissions Firewall helps you review and tighten controls for: 
- which services/regions/identities are used vs unused 
- which identities have direct entitlements to what permissions (used vs. unused)
